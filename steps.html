Step-1 :

Registration:

Registration is the initial step in using "Go Cycle." Users need to download the mobile app and create an account. During this process, they provide their name, contact information, and payment preferences, ensuring a seamless and secure experience. Users may also need to accept the terms and conditions of use. This registration step establishes a user's identity and creates a secure connection between the user and the "Go Cycle" service, enabling them to access the rental bicycles.

Step-2 :

Locating Bicycles:

After registering and logging into the mobile app, users can access a map that displays the real-time locations of "Go Cycle" bicycles in their vicinity. This map helps users identify the nearest available bicycles and check their availability status. It enables users to make an informed decision about which bicycle to rent and where to start their journey. The real-time location feature ensures that users can easily find and access a nearby bicycle, contributing to the convenience and efficiency of the service.

Step-3 :

QR Code Scanning:

To initiate the rental process, users select the desired bicycle from the app's interface. They then use their smartphone's camera to scan the unique QR code located on the bicycle. Scanning the QR code is a critical security measure, as it establishes a connection between the user's account and the chosen bicycle. This ensures that the user is renting the intended bicycle and prevents unauthorized access.

❮ ❯

Step-4 :

Generating OTP:

After successfully scanning the QR code, the app generates a one-time password (OTP) and sends it to the user's mobile device. The OTP is a temporary code used to confirm the rental and enhance security. Users must enter this OTP into the app to validate their rental, adding an extra layer of security to the process.

❮ ❯

Step-5 :

Unlocking the Bicycle:

With the OTP confirmed, users can unlock the chosen bicycle using the app. The app sends a signal to the bicycle's lock, releasing it and allowing the user to start their ride. This step ensures that only authorized users can unlock the bicycles and initiate their rental. It contributes to the security and reliability of the service.

Step-6 :

Riding and Parking:

Once the bicycle is unlocked, users can begin their ride. The app tracks their ride duration and distance, providing real-time information to users. Users can enjoy their journey to their destination. At the end of the ride, they are guided to park the bicycle at a designated "Go Cycle" parking stand. This step ensures that users can securely and conveniently park the bicycle, promoting organized and safe parking practices.

Step-7 :

End of Ride:

At the conclusion of their ride, users must use the app to finalize their session. The app calculates the rental fee based on the ride duration, distance covered, and any additional fees, providing users with a transparent and accurate payment summary. Users confirm the end of their ride, making the bicycle available for other users. This step ensures that the rental process is completed and that the bicycle can be used by the next rider.

Step-8 :

Locking the Bicycle:

After concluding the ride, users must lock the bicycle securely using the app. The app sends a signal to the bicycle's lock, securing it in place. Locking the bicycle ensures that it is available for the next user, and it is a critical step in maintaining the availability and security of the bicycle fleet. This step contributes to the efficient operation of the service.

Step-9 :

Security and Maintenance:

"Go Cycle" places a strong emphasis on security and maintenance to ensure user safety and the reliability of the service. The bicycles are regularly inspected and undergo maintenance to address wear and tear. Additionally, security features like QR code scanning and OTPs are implemented to prevent unauthorized use and enhance the overall security of the service. These measures are fundamental to providing a safe and secure riding experience for users.

Step-10 :

Payment:

The payment for the ride is processed through the user's preferred payment method, which was set up during registration. The app calculates the rental fee based on the duration and distance traveled during the ride. Users receive a transparent payment summary, and the payment is securely processed. This step ensures a seamless and hassle-free payment process, providing users with clarity and convenience when finalizing their ride. The financial aspect of the rental process is crucial for a smooth and user-friendly experience.
